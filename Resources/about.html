COD4 Map Tactics is all about Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare maps and tactics for each map. This app has ALL the maps, including maps from Variety Map Pack.

Features

20 high quality maps for all the maps of the game (including the the variety map pack).

Maps can be zoomed in and out either by pinching or double-tapping.

Tips and tactics for every map whether you are a run 'n gun type, or a camper, or sniper or ninja.

Will add videos soon featuring tactics from famous players of the COD4 game.
